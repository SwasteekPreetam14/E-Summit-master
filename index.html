About Us

A Entrepreneurship summit is Usually the annual gatherings of different entrepreneurs. These global leaders from multiple regions of the world engage diverse groups of audience through presentations. If it is executed well, these summits provide value to the participants. The representatives of government bodies, industry leaders, investors, Entrepreneurs offer presentations depicting present scenario and potential avenues for the students in their respective fields of study.

Entrepreneurship Summit at VSSUT will provide valuable input to the participatants from in and around of the university. Students will be able to participate in various kind of events that are going to be organized by the Entrepreneurship Cell, VSSUT. Following events are going to be organized on the eve of Entrepreneurship Summit 2020 -

Leadership Summit

Leadership Summit is the annual gathering of different oranganisations' leaders.

VSSUT InnoBuzz Dwitiya

InnoBuzz is the second edition of the Largest In-House Hackathon of India.

Start-Up Bootcamp

Start-Up Bootcamp is being oragnized in order to bring up the Startup culture in the locality.

Women Entrepreneurship Workshop

The Workshop serves as a platform for emerging women entrepreneurs and other stakeholders to discuss the key challenges facing women entrepreneurs.

Green Marathon

To contribute towards the environment, E-cell is going to organise 'Green Marathon' to showcase for a sustainable surrounding.

Cultural Nights

Standup Comedy and Slam Poetry are being organized for the attendees to relax their minds.
